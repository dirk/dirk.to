Dirk Gadsden

Is a software engineer. He has worked with companies such as Fuzzco, Indigo Strategies, Indie Grits, Fetchnotes, and more on projects ranging from web apps to festivals (not just one) to non-profits to a variety of other large and small websites, wep apps, and mobile apps.

Besides commercial work he also contributes to open-source (with a certain passion for virtual machines and programming languages). Outside programming he enjoys a good run, cooking nigh-endless bowls of vegetarian tomato pasta, and hacking on the cutting edge of programming.
